About me

A bibliophile is a person who loves or collects books, especially as examples of fine or unusual printing, binding, or the like1. The word "bibliophile" comes from the Greek prefix biblio, meaning "book," and the word philos, meaning "friend"2. Bibliophiles love books particularly for how they look, how they smell, and what they feel like3. They often amass a large and specialized collection of books they love or that hold special value, including old editions with unusual bindings, autographed, or illustrated copies4
